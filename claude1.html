FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers. Here are the most common ones we hear.

What is included in the monthly rent?
Monthly rent includes fully furnished accommodation, three meals a day (breakfast, lunch, and dinner), high-speed WiFi, electricity (within fair usage), housekeeping, water supply, and access to all common amenities including gym and study area.
Is there a security deposit? Is it refundable?
Yes, a refundable security deposit of 1–2 months' rent is collected at the time of joining. This is fully refunded within 7–14 working days after vacating, subject to property condition checks with no damage deductions.
What are the visiting hours and gate timings?
For Aster Homes (Boys): gate open 24/7 with a check-in log after 10 PM. For Iris Homes (Girls): entry until 10 PM; prior intimation required for late returns. Visitors are welcome in common areas between 9 AM – 8 PM only.
Are meals customizable for dietary preferences?
Yes! We cater to vegetarian, non-vegetarian, vegan, and Jain diets. Residents can also request special meal plans or inform the kitchen of allergies. We believe good nutrition is essential for your well-being.
What is the minimum lock-in period?
The minimum stay is 3 months. A 30-day notice period is required before vacating. Short-term stays of 1–2 months can be arranged on request at a slightly higher rate.
How is the safety and security managed?
We have 24/7 security guards, CCTV cameras covering all common areas and entry/exit points, biometric/OTP-based access control, and a resident warden available round the clock. Iris Homes additionally has a dedicated lady warden on-site.
Can I bring my own furniture or appliances?
Rooms are fully furnished and you're welcome to add personal items like small fans, table lamps, or décor. Large appliances like personal refrigerators require prior approval. Cooking in rooms is not permitted for safety reasons.
How do I book a room or schedule a visit?
Simply WhatsApp or call us using the contact details below. You can schedule an in-person property tour (recommended before booking) or request a virtual walkthrough. We respond within 30 minutes during working hours.
