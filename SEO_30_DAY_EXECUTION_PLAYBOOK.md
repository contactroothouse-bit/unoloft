# Unoloft 30-Day SEO Execution Playbook

## Goal
Rank higher for local commercial-intent keywords:
- `pg near gota`
- `pg near nirma university`
- `pg in gota`
- `best pg in gota`
- `best pg in ahmedabad`

This plan is execution-first. Follow it daily for 30 days.

## North-Star KPIs (Track Weekly)
- Google Search Console clicks for location pages
- Impressions and average position for target keywords
- Google Business Profile calls, direction requests, and website clicks
- Number of new reviews (and review text quality)
- Number of new referring domains/backlinks

## Priority Pages
- `/`
- `/pg-in-gota-ahmedabad`
- `/pg-near-nirma-university`
- `/pg-near-sg-highway`
- `/aster-homes`
- `/iris-house`

## Daily Non-Negotiables (Days 1-30)
- Reply to all new Google reviews within 24 hours.
- Publish at least one fresh image/video on Google Business Profile.
- Track rankings manually for target keywords in incognito (Ahmedabad location).
- Check Search Console for indexing and crawl issues.

## Week 1 (Days 1-7): Local SEO Foundation + Indexing Push

### Day 1
- Verify both Google Business Profiles are fully complete:
  - Primary category: `Boys' hostel` or closest accurate option
  - Secondary categories: `Paying guest accommodation`, `Student housing center` (if available)
  - Services and amenities filled
  - Phone, address, hours, website URL all consistent with site
- Add UTM tags to website links in GBP:
  - `https://www.unoloft.com/?utm_source=google&utm_medium=organic&utm_campaign=gbp_aster`
  - `https://www.unoloft.com/?utm_source=google&utm_medium=organic&utm_campaign=gbp_iris`

### Day 2
- Submit indexing requests in Google Search Console for:
  - `/`
  - `/pg-in-gota-ahmedabad`
  - `/pg-near-nirma-university`
  - `/aster-homes`
  - `/iris-house`
- Re-submit sitemap: `https://www.unoloft.com/sitemap.xml`

### Day 3
- Upload 10 geotargeted GBP photos (building, rooms, meals, study area, entry gate).
- File naming pattern:
  - `pg-in-gota-ahmedabad-unoloft-room-1.jpg`
  - `pg-near-nirma-university-unoloft-study-room.jpg`

### Day 4
- Ask 10 recent residents for reviews using WhatsApp template (see templates section).
- Ask for natural mention of location phrases:
  - "near Nirma University"
  - "PG in Gota"
  - "best PG in Ahmedabad" (only if true to their experience)

### Day 5
- Publish 1 GBP post:
  - Topic: "Why students choose Unoloft near Nirma University"
  - CTA: "Call now" or "Learn more"

### Day 6
- Add 5 local citations (consistent NAP):
  - JustDial
  - Sulekha
  - IndiaMART
  - Housing/student listing websites
  - Ahmedabad local business directory

### Day 7
- Review week metrics:
  - Pages indexed?
  - Any keyword movement?
  - How many reviews added?
  - Number of calls/leads from GBP?

## Week 2 (Days 8-14): Topical Authority + Internal Link Boost

### Content Publishing (2 pieces this week)
- Publish one blog targeting `pg near gota`.
- Publish one blog targeting `best pg in ahmedabad`.
- Must include:
  - target keyword in title
  - target keyword in first paragraph
  - internal links to `/pg-in-gota-ahmedabad` and `/pg-near-nirma-university`
  - one FAQ block with intent-rich question

### Internal Linking Tasks
- Add contextual links from new blogs to:
  - `/rooms`
  - `/amenities`
  - `/contact`
- Keep anchor texts varied but relevant:
  - "premium PG in Gota"
  - "PG near Nirma University Ahmedabad"
  - "best PG rooms in Ahmedabad"

### GBP + Review Tasks
- 2 GBP posts this week (1 offer/update + 1 educational).
- Request 10 more reviews.
- Add 1 short video tour to GBP.

## Week 4 (Days 22-30): Optimize What Works

### Search Console Optimization
- Identify queries with high impressions but low CTR.
- Update title/description for low-CTR pages:
  - include stronger benefit words (AC rooms, meals, 24/7 security, near Nirma).

### Conversion Optimization
- Add stronger CTA placement on top-performing pages.
- Keep WhatsApp CTA visible in first screen and after key sections.

### GBP Optimization
- Add fresh weekly post.
- Add "Rooms & Amenities" photo album.
- Continue review velocity (minimum 2-3 new per week).

### Final Day (Day 30) Audit
- Ranking changes for all five target keywords.
- Click growth vs Day 1 baseline.
- Indexed pages count.
- New reviews count and average rating.
- Referring domains growth.
- Top 3 pages by leads/calls.
